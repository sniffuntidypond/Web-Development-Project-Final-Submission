About Us

Some information about who we are


4:3 Studio is a media company created by Oswald Chong in 2023.
He first designed the studio so his novel can come under one publication label.
4:3 Studio is much more than just novels.
It has evolved into other mediums of entertainment like film and Television.
All films and TV shows created by Oswald Chong will come under the publication label of 4:3 Studio.
